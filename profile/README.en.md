Hangzhou No. 2 High School Butian Engineering Club is a student organization focused on aerospace engineering and space science.

We explore topics such as space station design, deep-space exploration, space settlement, resource utilization, energy systems, and engineering management. Through project-based research, system design, competitions, and outreach, we turn scientific curiosity into structured, engineering-driven solutions.

Members develop not only technical knowledge, but also skills in problem analysis, technical writing, visual presentation, teamwork, and project execution. We aim to approach space with scientific rigor and move toward the future with solid engineering thinking.
